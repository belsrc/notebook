$$
\sum_{i=1}^{5} i^2 = 
1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 + 4^2 + 5^2 =
1 + 4 + 9 + 16 + 25
$$

$$
\prod_{i=1}^{5} i^2 = 
1^2 \times 2^2 \times 3^2 \times 4^2 \times 5^2 = 
1 \times 4 \times 9 \times 16 \times 25
$$
